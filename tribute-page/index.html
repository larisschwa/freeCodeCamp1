Lights

Canadian Musician

Here are some of her musical accomplishments:

  • 2006-07 - She began her career writing for Sony/ATV Music Publishing
  • 2008-09 Lights, The Listening - Began touring the United States and Canada
  • 2010-13 Acoustic EP, Siberia, Siberia Acoustic - Toured worldwide with Owl City
  • 2014-16 Little Machines - Toured extensively to promote Little Machines. Also released Midnight Machines which featured acoustic versions of some songs
  • 2017-Present Skin and Earth, Dead End - Released Skin and Earth with accompanying comic book series. Also released remix album Dead End
